National Insurance Nepal is Foreign branch of National Insurance company limited(India), which is public sector non life insurance company regulated by Ministry of Finance, Government of India

The journey started in Nepal in 1959 as Rubi General Insurance & Hindustan General Insurance. After Nationalization of insurance companies in India in 1972, 32 insurance companies merged into one and started operating as National insurance company limited.
