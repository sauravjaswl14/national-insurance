A Few Words About Us

National Insurance Company Limited is the Centennial Insurance Company in India operating since 1906. It is one of the leading Public Sector Non Life Insurance Company of India, Headquartered in Kolkata, having only foreign branch office in Nepal. NICL is serving Nepal since 1959 under the banner of Rubi General Insurance & Hindustan General Insurance. After Nationalization in India, Company got its registration from Beema Samiti in 1974 in the name of National Insurance Company Limited. NICL operates in Nepal with strong Financials and retention capacity of its own, thereby having less dependence of reinsuring the risk abroad resulting in speedy claim settlement with the highest deposit/ investment base among the non- life insurers. At present we have our Controlling office in Kathmandu with two Branch Office and One Controlling unit looking after RI, Claim and Marketing activities. We have a Centralized Claims Processing Hub (CCH) in Kathmandu for speedy and hassle free settlement of entire Nepal operations which came in operation with effect from 01.04.2012. Apart from this, we have Nine offices outside valley to provide the Insurance related services to all our Customers. Being an India Base company we have an advantage to serve Business communities of Nepal who regularly deal with Indian companies and vendors.
